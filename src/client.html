Our Work

Method

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with an in-depth discussion to understand your company’s needs, goals, and the specific roles you’re looking to fill. This helps us identify the ideal candidate profile and craft a recruitment strategy tailored to your business.

2

Role Definition & Market Research

Our team refines the job description and conducts market research to ensure the role is competitive and appealing. We then identify key channels to source top talent.

3

Candidate Search & Screening

Using our extensive network and recruitment tools, we source highly qualified candidates. We conduct initial screenings, including interviews, skill assessments, and background checks, to shortlist the best fits for your needs.

4

Candidate Presentation

We provide a detailed profile of each shortlisted candidate, including their experience, skills, and a summary of why they’re a great match for your company.

5

Client Interviews

We coordinate interviews between you and the selected candidates. Our team remains on hand to offer guidance and gather feedback throughout this stage.

6

Offer & Negotiation

Once you’ve chosen your ideal candidate, we assist in extending the offer, negotiating terms, and ensuring a smooth acceptance process.

7

Onboarding Support

We stay involved even after the hire, offering guidance to both you and the candidate during the onboarding phase to ensure a seamless transition and long-term success.

8

Follow-Up

Our relationship doesn’t end at placement. We check in periodically to ensure satisfaction and provide ongoing support as needed.
